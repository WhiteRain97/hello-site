FAQs

Common Questions

What does a remodel cost in Dallas?

It depends on scope and finishes, but you'll see a full fixed price in our proposal — no moving targets.

How long will my project take?

Kitchens typically run 8–12 weeks; whole-home remodels 4–8 months. We share a written schedule before we start.

Do you really offer fixed pricing?

Yes. Change orders only happen if you change the scope.

What does your warranty cover?

1 year on workmanship, 2 years on major systems, and 10 years on structural.

Can we live in our home during the remodel?

Usually yes — we plan phasing and dust control around your daily life.

What's the first step?

Book a consultation. We'll walk the space, talk goals, and outline what's possible.

Ready to start your project?

Tell us about your home. We'll come back with a real plan and a real price.
