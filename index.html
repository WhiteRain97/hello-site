FAQs

Your Remodeling Questions, Answered

What does a remodel cost in Dallas?

It depends on scope and finishes, but you'll see a full fixed price in our proposal — no moving targets. Our 2026 DFW cost guide breaks down typical ranges by project type.

How long will my project take?

Kitchens typically run 8–12 weeks; whole-home remodels 4–8 months. We share a written schedule before we start.

Do you really offer fixed pricing?

Yes. We finalize plans and selections before construction so we can give you a real, fixed price. Change orders only happen if you change the scope.

What does your warranty cover?

1 year on workmanship, 2 years on major systems, and 10 years on structural — written into every contract.

Can we live in our home during the remodel?

Usually yes — we plan phasing and dust control around your daily life. For some whole-home projects, temporary housing is the cleaner option.

What's the first step to booking a consultation?

Fill out the estimate form or call (214) 972-6402. We'll set up a no-pressure visit to your home.
